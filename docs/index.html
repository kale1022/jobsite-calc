Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

JobSite Calc does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no network requests made by the app.

  • Calculations happen entirely on your device and are not saved after you leave a calculator.
  • Purchases are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. We never see your payment information. Purchase state is stored only on your device and in your Apple account.

Because the app collects nothing, there is nothing to delete, sell, or share. If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page before taking effect.
